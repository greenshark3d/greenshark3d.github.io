The SME Manufacturing Dilemma

Tier 2 and Tier 3 suppliers are heavily pressured by large automotive clients to deliver zero-defect parts. However, traditional industrial testing rigs (e.g., Siemens, Bosch) cost upwards of €50,000. Lacking the budget, many SMEs rely on slow, error-prone manual testing, completely lacking digital traceability.

Our IIoT Approach

1

Custom 3D-Printed Fixtures

We design and FDM-print bespoke mechanical mounts tailored to specific production parts, drastically reducing hardware costs.

2

Accessible Microcontrollers

Powered by ESP32/Arduino logic and analog sensors. We measure resistance, force, and continuity with industrial accuracy.

3

Wi-Fi Data Logging

Goodbye manual Excel sheets. Data is pushed in real-time via Wi-Fi to a web dashboard for live monitoring and predictive maintenance.
